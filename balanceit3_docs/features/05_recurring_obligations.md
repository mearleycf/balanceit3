# Recurring Obligations

## Purpose

Recurring obligations define the required outflows that must be protected before discretionary spending is considered safe.

## Core Capabilities

- create and maintain obligation schedules
- support common cadences
- track due, late, skipped, and changed states
- expose missed or uncertain obligations to reconciliation and alerts

## Key Requirements

- `FR-13` through `FR-15`
- `FR-16` through `FR-19`

## Why It Matters

This feature materially shapes both forecast low points and the final safe-to-spend amount.
